June 2024 Newsletter
Congratulations on another great year of Science Olympiad! From late night study sessions to last-minute build modifications, we showed our dedication throughout the year, and the work paid off! This year was by far our most exciting and successful season. We want to extend our gratitude to all the teachers, families, and participants who made this remarkable season possible.

Updates and Information
Season Recap
This year, our team excelled in competitions with an impressive record: 10 first-place finishes, 12 second-place, 14 third-place, 18 fourth-place, 8 fifth-place, and 7 sixth-place achievements. These results highlight our dedication and competitive edge, setting a strong foundation for future success.
Season Highlights
We earned a total of 3 trophies and 71 medals!

We are also extremely proud of our team and most notably, our performance at the Fairfax Regional where we secured FIRST place!
End of Year Party
As our school year comes to a close, we celebrated with an eventful end of year party filled with fun, bonding, and water balloons!
What's Next
Over the summer, our team plans to stay connected and continue the grind. Feel free to get ahead; there is no hurt in doing a little studying over the summer to prepare yourself for an amazing season next year. We will continue to send future newsletters to update our WSO families and students about tryouts, bonding activities, and more.

Have a wonderful summer and once again, thank you for an incredible year!
